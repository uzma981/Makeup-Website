Natural Glam Red Lip

This look consists of using minimal products on your skin to create the most natural , radiant glow to your face. This look is ideal if you’re wanting to enhance your natural features, which consists of a simple eyeliner, curled lashes with a bold red lipstick to catch the attention. The real focus will be on the highlighter and the red lipstick, therefore opting for a waterbased foundation or even a tinted moisturiser would be ideal as it sinks into the skin giving it the overall natural glam.

Soft and Pink

There are many shades of pink, so picking the right one is essential for yourself. Starting off with a light pink and slowly building up for the transition shade allows you take your time to blend in the pink eyeshadows together. Choosing a softer pink/coral blush will also add some extra ‘barbie’ like feeling and give your cheeks a little tint to it. Finally ending It with a transparent gloss or a nude lipstick will create an even distribution of shades across your face. 

Brown smokey bronzed

If youre going on a date night, or even feeling bold the brown smokey look is the one for you. Applying bronzer all over your cheeks and forehead gives the illusion of a smaller and more of a heart shaped face. It also gives you a tanned look and you can top it off with a pink liquid lipstick. The real focus will be on the eyes where you can add hint of gold to the inner corners of your eyes.
