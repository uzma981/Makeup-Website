Introduction to Makeup&Co

This website is based on giving you an insight on the makeup world that we are living in today. Makeup does not define your beauty and you should be confident in your own skin and use makeup to enhance your beauty. You should always take care of your skin before piling on foundation as you need to keep it young and fresh with daily skin care. Try investing in cheap drugstore makeup before diving in to the expensive high end products that will blow your purse. I would highly recommend starting with a small makeup kit before building on a collection of foundations and concealers. We will first focus on my best recommendations for affordable products to start off with.

Affordable Makeup products:

  1. Maybelline Fit Me Liquid Foundation- (£5.99)- One of the famous drugstore foundation includes the Fit me foundation which is perfect for any skin type and shade. They have many shades in stock to suit you perfectly. It gives a natural matte finish and provides a poreless looking skin.
  2. Revolution Conceal and Define (£4) One of the best concealers for under eyes to conceal and define around the cheek bones. This concealer creates a blur effects on your skin for under £5, so its worth a buy!
  3. Maybelline Lash Sensational Mascara Intense Black (£6.99)- This mascara is affordable and delivers the best result for your lashes even if you have non- existent ones like mine. The want is nice and thick to create the most voluminous look and to pick up every last lash. It does not clump and gives a curved finish to the finest hairs.
  4. Sleek Contour Kit - (£6.99) This is one of the most famous product from the Sleek brank as it includes a duo contour powder and a highlighter in one. The shimmery highlighter adds dimension to the cheekbone and illuminated the face, while the contour adds that finishing touch to the cheekbone. This palllete is a go to for daily makeup and is worth the price since you dont have to rebuy another highlighter.
  5. Rimmel Maxi Blush (£5.95)- This is hands down the best blusher i have every used, it creates a luminous, radiant complexion to the skin. There are many shades in this blush selection so you can choose a colour that best suits you. It is highly pigmented where little goes a long way. This blusher is definitely worth a buy.
  6. NYX Soft Matte Lip cream (£6)- To complete your look, a nude lipstick is necessary. Here i have opted for the lip cream in Milan as it a pigmented shade and so soft on your lips. For daily makeup either a gloss or a lip cream is preffered over a matte liquid lipstick which can dry out your lips.
